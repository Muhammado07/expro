About Kash Program

Kash program is a sales incentive program brought by York to give you the opportunity to become you own boss and increase your income. You can share your customized promo-code to generate sales. With every product sold using your promo-code you’ll receive an incentive.

How it works?

  1. Register

    Fill in you information by clicking "Join Now"

  2. Promo Code

    Choose your own Promo Code to share with your family and clients

  3. Acceptance Email

    An email with your application status will be sent along with your promo code when accepted.

  4. Start Selling

    Hit two birds with one stone! An incentive for you and a special discount for them from YORK

  5. Monitor your Sales

    At any time & place, monitor your sales using your customized dashboard by signing in to your account.
